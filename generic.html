A Live Dice Game

❮ ❯

This project was made with html,css and javascript.The dice game came from the idea of the known popular ludo game which can accomodate four active players, which two dice are being rolled continullay physically until a player completes his or her winning task.At the end of each collective dice roll,the player advances into the line of the ludo board until the player wins the contest.

This game is similar to the popular ludo game you know only it can only consist of two active players,in which at the end of each dice roll,scores/points are being recorded until a player reaches the winning score in which he's declared a winner.
